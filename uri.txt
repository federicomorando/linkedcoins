ids.linkedcoins.org/ identifying things and concepts: it redirects to data or pages, depending on user agent;
data.linkedcoins.org/ serving RDF triples;
pages.linkedcoins.org/ serving HTML pages.

ids.linkedcoins.org/ontology/{ClassOrProperty} includes all ontology elements, classes and properties
ids.linkedcoins.org/resource/{Name} includes all the instances of resources



Example:
ids.linkedcoins.org/ontology/reference/Babelon/[$GENS]-[#]
ids.linkedcoins.org/ontology/roman/republic/[$GENS]-[#*100]

ids.linkedcoins.org/ontology/ReferenceId an identified within a standard reference/catalogue
